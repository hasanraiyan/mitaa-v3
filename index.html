About Us

Fostering a Lifelong Community

The Muzaffarpur Institute of Technology Alumni Association (MITAA) is committed to building a vibrant network that benefits alumni, supports current students, and contributes to the growth and prestige of MIT. We organize events, facilitate networking, and provide resources to keep our community connected and thriving.
